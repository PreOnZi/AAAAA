Do not abandon reduction trolleys after use. Dispose off products and return stickers back to the original place.

Date-check:

  • 12pm – 30%
  • 4pm – 50%
  • 7pm – 75%

What is NOT reduced 30%, but is reduced with further reductions:

  • Food to go (sandwiches, sushi,…)
  • Pre-sliced fresh fruit
  • Flowers

Other Reductions:

  • Damaged ambient that has not been exposed – still safe to sell
  • Damaged cereal box, split crisp bag with individual bags intact, missing bar from a chocolate bar, etc.
  • Specials only reduced upon manager approval
