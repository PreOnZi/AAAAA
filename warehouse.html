Pumptrucks:

  • LONG = Two stripes on top
  • SHORT = One stripe in the middle

Empties:

  • Stack of up to 11 Red EU pallets – marked as LPR collected together
  • Stack of up to 11 Blue EU pallets
  • Stack of up to 11 brown/non-LPR red/white EU pallets
  • UK pallets on top of bale ideally
  • D-pallets separated black and grey, 12 high
  • Drinks platforms – separated standalone/ on top of a smaller d-pallet stack
  • Produce/chill green crates, 35 high on an LPR red pallet
  • Bread smaller: Not to go above 190cm
  • Bread larger: Not to go above 190cm
  • Flower buckets – emptied of water, in a potato shipper/wrapped on a d-pallet
  • Batteries – together with flower buckets
  • D-pallets, produce crates, bread crate all need to be WRAPPED

Loading bay:

  • Only leave empties ready to be returned to the warehouse
  • Green crates on a red pallet are only collected when all 4 spaces are fully occupied and wrapped
  • Do not mix drink platforms

Chiller:

  • Short life:
    • Keep older products in front of the newer ones to maintain rotation
    • Try keeping older products on top of newer ones as well
  • Long life:
    • Older cages on the right
